DIASPORA* Easyshare

Mozilla Firefox add-on to easily share web content on your Diaspora* pod.
https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/diaspora-easyshare/

Settings

After install you must save the URL of your Diaspora* pod.
Go to Firefox > Addons > Extensions > Diaspora* Easyshare > Preferences
and enter the URL (e.g. https://yourpod.com).

Note:
There's a little tedious bug when typing the url (Bug 784335):
Typing forward slash ("/") in the preference field moves cursor to search field. You need to put again the cursor in the field.

Usage

Share images
Right click on images and choose Share on Diaspora*. Selected text is automatically entered.
Share selection
Share only selected text. Right click and choose Share selection on Diaspora*.
Share videos
Share videos from youtube.com. Right click on video page and choose Share video on Diaspora*. Video description is automatically entered.
You must be logged to your Diaspora* pod to use this addon.

The panel

Title
Shows document-title/video-title. You can edit or change it.
Url
Shows document-URL/video-URL. You can shorten long urls.
Text
Automatically shows text selected or video description but you can insert what you want. You can add markdown emphasis (italics) or use your own markdown syntax.
Tags
Hashtags. Enter them separated by commas.

The post

Diaspora* Easyshare send the post to Diaspora via the publisher bookmarklet, forcing it to build the post as:
image/title/link/text/hashtags/ - to share images
title/link/text/hashtags/ - to share text-only
title/text/hashtags/link/video/ - to share videos.

The widget

You can send fast text-only posts using the add-on bar widget. The widget can be permanently removed by the preferences.

Preferences

Firefox > Addons > Extensions > Diaspora* Easyshare > Preferences
Pod URL (required)
The URL of your diaspora* pod.
Widget
Show/remove the add-on bar widget. If you remove it and want to show again in the same Firefox session you must restart the browser.
Advertises
Add/remove "via Easyshare" at end of post.
Set the publisher window height
If you have many aspects you can increase the publisher window height.
Your Bitly API credentials
Uses your own Bitly API credentials to shorten URLs or leave the fields blank if you want use the internal ones.
