OpenID registration and authorization

After installation the addon must be registered and authorized starting an implicit flow to get a valid access token.

Refresh the access token

The lifetime of the access token is 24 hours. When the pod responds with a 401 error (Unauthorized) or the status indicates that the token is expired you need to authorize the addon again to get a new valid access token.
